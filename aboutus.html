About Us

Bamboo Hosting

Bamboo Hosting was started in April 2018 in Chicago. We live where you live, we work where you work, right in the beautiful and mighty Windy City. Our goal is to be the best small local web hosting. We are the opposite of the big web hosts, we are a local neighbor, here to connect you to the online world.

Our diverse clients range from a pho restaurant to an auto body shop to a dental office to photographers and film makers. We provide the personal service and support you could only get from a smart techy friend. How many web hosts can you text to get an answer within minutes? Running a small business is hard, let us worry about your website so you can focus on growing.
